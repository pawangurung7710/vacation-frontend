# SERP Intelligence Research Skill

## When to use this skill
- When planning new content
- When competitors outrank you
- When AI answers not citing your brand

## How to use this skill
1. **Top 10 SERP Intent Mapping**  
   Analyze search results for user intent, featured snippets, and questions asked.  
2. **Competitor Gap Analysis**  
   Identify content and topic gaps that competitors cover but you don’t.  
3. **FAQ & Snippet Targets**  
   Extract common questions and answer formats to structure your own pages.  
4. **AI SERP Visibility**  
   Track how often your brand / competitors appear in AI answer summaries.

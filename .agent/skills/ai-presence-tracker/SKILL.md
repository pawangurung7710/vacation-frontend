# AI Presence Tracker Skill

## When to use this skill
- Ongoing SEO monitoring
- When expanding content strategy
- When evaluating competitive visibility

## How to use this skill
1. **AI Citation Metrics**  
   Measure how often your brand is referenced in AI responses.  
2. **Visibility Trends**  
   Track changes over time across systems.  
3. **Competitive Share of Voice**  
   Compare how often competitors are cited by LLMs vs. your brand.  
4. **Action Plan Integration**  
   Use findings to refine content gaps and priorities.

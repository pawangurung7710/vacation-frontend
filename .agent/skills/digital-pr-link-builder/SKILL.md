# Digital PR & Link Builder Skill

## When to use this skill
- When domain authority (DR) is low compared to competitors.
- After core money pages (EBC, ABC) are fully optimized and published.
- For promoting new unique trekking packages, safety reports, or ultimate guides.

## How to use this skill
1. **Unlinked Brand Mentions**
   Identify where the brand is mentioned in travel blogs or news but not linked, and draft outreach emails to request a link.
2. **Niche Edits & Resource Pages**
   Find high-quality adventure travel or Nepal tourism resource pages and propose adding your comprehensive guides (e.g., "Complete Guide to EBC Permits").
3. **Digital PR Campaigns**
   Help structure data-driven PR pieces (e.g., "Trekking Safety Statistics in Nepal 2024") to pitch to travel journalists.
4. **Local & Niche Directories**
   Ensure the business is listed in top-tier local directories, international tourism boards, and trekking associations (TAAN, NMA).

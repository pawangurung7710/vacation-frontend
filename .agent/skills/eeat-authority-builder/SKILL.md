# E-E-A-T Authority Builder

## When to use this skill
- On About/Guide bio pages
- When publishing expert content
- When showcasing testimonials or case studies
- Ongoing reputation management

## How to use this skill
1. **Author Credentials**  
   Include detailed bios: years of experience, certifications, field stories, and personal photos.  
2. **Original Data & Insights**  
   Add unique observations, photos, field notes, and benchmarks not found elsewhere.  
3. **Trust Signals**  
   Display verified reviews from Google/Tripadvisor.  
4. **Reputation Engagement**  
   Prompt responses to reviews and maintain consistency across all brand listings.

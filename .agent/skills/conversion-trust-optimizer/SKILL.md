# Conversion & Trust Optimizer Skill

## When to use this skill
- On money pages (Trekking Packages, Contact forms).
- When traffic is increasing but booking inquiries are low.
- During the design phase of pricing sections and itineraries.

## How to use this skill
1. **Trust Signals Above the Fold**
   Ensure reviews (TripAdvisor, Trustpilot), licenses (TAAN, Gov of Nepal), and safety guarantees are highly visible before scrolling.
2. **Frictionless Inquiries**
   Optimize CTA buttons. Add sticky "Book Now" or "Inquire via WhatsApp" widgets that remain visible on mobile.
3. **Pricing Transparency**
   Structure pricing tables clearly. Outline exactly what is included vs. excluded to reduce user anxiety.
4. **Social Proof Integration**
   Embed real traveler video testimonials or user-generated content (UGC) near the conversion points of the page.

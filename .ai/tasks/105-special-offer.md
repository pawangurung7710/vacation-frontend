Special Offers / Deals Page

Project: Vacation Nepal Trekking
Goal: Showcase active trekking deals with urgency and professionalism
Phase: Frontend only (no backend logic)

🎯 Page Objective

Convert deal-driven visitors without damaging premium positioning.

Tone:

Exclusive

Limited-time

Curated

Not “cheap”

🧱 PAGE STRUCTURE
1️⃣ Hero Section (Simple & Focused)

Full-width but lighter than homepage.

H1:

“Exclusive Himalayan Trekking Offers”

Subtitle:
“Limited-time journeys crafted for unforgettable experiences.”

Optional:
Small urgency line:
“Updated for 2025 season departures”

CTA:
“View Active Offers”

2️⃣ Active Offers Section (Core Section)

Grid layout (2 or 3 columns desktop).

Each offer = Premium Card Design (consistent with trek cards).

🔥 Offer Card Structure
Image

High-quality trek image (not generic sale graphic)

Badge (Top corner)

Limited Time

Early Bird

Autumn Special

Small Group Offer

Title (H3)

“Everest Base Camp – Autumn Special”

Short Description

2–3 lines explaining value.

Offer Highlights

10% off standard price

Free airport transfer

Complimentary duffel bag

Free upgrade (if luxury tier)

Expiry Display (Important)

Clear visual:

“Offer valid until: October 31, 2025”

Use subtle red or accent highlight.

CTA Button

“Claim This Offer”
→ Scroll to inquiry form or open WhatsApp

3️⃣ Conditions Section (Transparency Builder)

Very important to protect brand trust.

Clean card or light background section.

Include:

Valid for specific departure months

Minimum group size required

Non-refundable deposit terms

Cannot combine with other promotions

Subject to availability

Tone:
Clear. Honest. No hidden surprises.

4️⃣ Urgency + Expiry Section

Dedicated visual section.

Example:

⏳ “These offers are available for a limited time.”

You can include:

Static expiry dates (since no backend)

Manual countdown design (optional, not dynamic)

Keep it elegant — avoid aggressive sales feel.

5️⃣ CTA Section (High Conversion Block)

Full-width clean section.

Headline:
“Ready to Secure Your Himalayan Adventure?”

Short line:
“Limited spaces available for seasonal departures.”

Buttons:

Inquire Now

WhatsApp Now

Optional reassurance:

✔ No hidden costs
✔ Local expert planning
✔ Response within 24 hours

🎨 Design Guidelines

Same typography as homepage

Same spacing rhythm

Same card shadow

Same button style

Consistent iconography

No flashy red sale graphics

No discount-heavy cheap appearance

Keep it premium.

Manually add/remove offers

No automatic deactivation

Simple, clean, controllable.

About Us Page

Project: Vacation Nepal Trekking
Goal: Establish trust, legitimacy, and premium positioning
Tone: Authoritative + Inspising + Transparent

🎯 Page Objective

When someone lands here, they should think:

“This is a real, professional, locally rooted, internationally capable trekking company.”

🧱 PAGE STRUCTURE
1️⃣ Hero Section – Trust First

Clean Himalayan background (less dramatic, more authentic).

H1:

“About Vacation Nepal Trekking”

Subtitle:

“Local expertise. International standards. Himalayan passion.”

Optional:
Short credibility line:
“Based in Kathmandu, operating across Nepal’s major trekking regions.”

2️⃣ Company Story (Core Emotional Section)

Two-column layout:

Left: Story Content

Tell:

How the company started

Founder’s trekking experience

Passion for the Himalayas

Why the company was created

Commitment to safety & authenticity

Tone:
Inspiring but professional.

Avoid:
Too dramatic storytelling.

Right: Supporting Visual

Founder photo OR

Authentic trekking team image

OR office image (adds legitimacy)

3️⃣ Mission & Values Section

Clean icon-based layout (4–6 pillars).

Suggested Values:

🏔 Safety First
🌍 Responsible Tourism
🤝 Local Employment
⭐ Premium Service Standards
💬 Transparent Pricing
🌱 Sustainable Practices

Short 1–2 line explanation under each.

Keep it structured and clear.

4️⃣ Registration & Legal Details (Trust Anchor Section)

This section separates you from small informal operators.

Use a light background or card layout.

Include:

Company Registration Number

PAN/VAT Number

Registered Address (City level, not personal detail)

Trekking Agency Association membership

Insurance coverage statement

Government & Association Affiliations

You can mention:

Nepal Tourism Board

Trekking Agencies’ Association of Nepal (TAAN)

Ministry of Culture, Tourism & Civil Aviation

Display:
Logos (clean, grayscale or subtle)

Tone:
Professional, not boastful.

5️⃣ Timeline (Optional but Powerful)

Simple horizontal or vertical timeline.

Example:

2015 – Company founded
2017 – Expanded to Everest region operations
2019 – Introduced luxury trekking packages
2022 – Served 1,000+ international trekkers
2024 – Expanded custom expedition services

This builds long-term credibility.

6️⃣ Meet Our Team (Very Important)

This builds huge trust for international clients.

Grid layout (professional cards).

Each team card includes:

Professional photo

Name

Role (Lead Guide, Cultural Guide, Operations Manager)

Years of experience

Special expertise (Everest specialist, high-altitude rescue trained)

Languages spoken

Keep bios short and confident.

Avoid:
Overly casual descriptions.

7️⃣ CSR & Community Initiatives

This strengthens premium + ethical positioning.

Possible content:

Supporting local schools

Hiring local porters fairly

Responsible waste management

Eco-friendly trekking practices

Supporting remote mountain communities

Tone:
Responsible and genuine.

Not exaggerated.

Full-width clean section.

Headline:
“Ready to Trek with Trusted Himalayan Experts?”

Buttons:

Reinforce:

✔ Licensed & Registered

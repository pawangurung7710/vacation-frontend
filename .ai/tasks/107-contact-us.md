Contact Us Page

Project: Vacation Nepal Trekking
Goal: Provide clear, multi-channel communication for premium travelers
Tone: Professional + Helpful + Responsive

🎯 Page Objective

Make it extremely easy for potential clients to reach out via their preferred method (WhatsApp, Form, Email, or Phone) while reinforcing trust.

🧱 PAGE STRUCTURE
1️⃣ Hero Section
- Clean, minimalist background (e.g., Kathmandu office or a soft Himalayan view).
- H1: “Get in Touch”
- Subtitle: “Our local experts are ready to turn your Himalayan dreams into a reality.”

2️⃣ Contact Methods Grid (Core Section)
- 3 or 4 Column layout with premium cards.
- 💬 WhatsApp: Real-time consultation link.
- 📧 Email: Official business email.
- 📞 Phone: International & Local contact numbers.
- 📍 Address: Physical office location in Kathmandu.

3️⃣ Inquiry Form (Conversion Anchor)
- Professional, clean form design.
- Fields:
  - Full Name
  - Email Address
  - WhatsApp / Phone
  - Subject (Dropdown: General Inquiry / Booking / Custom Trip / Partnerships)
  - Message Area
- CTA: “Send Message”
- Success state: “Thank you! Our team will respond within 24 hours.”

4️⃣ Frequently Asked Questions (Quick Answers)
- Accordion or grid of 4-6 top questions (Booking process, Payment, Best time to visit, Safety).
- This reduces redundant inquiries.

5️⃣ Visit Our Office / Map Section
- Simple Google Maps embed or localized map graphic.
- Office hours (Monday - Friday).

6️⃣ Social Trust Section
- Links to Instagram, Facebook, and TripAdvisor/Trustpilot (placeholders).

📈 SEO Structure
H1: Contact Vacation Nepal Trekking
H2s: 
- Send Us a Message
- Our Kathmandu Office
- Contact Details
- Frequently Asked Questions

Target Keywords:
- Contact Nepal trekking agency
- Nepal trekking office Kathmandu
- Book Nepal trek
- Himalayan trekking experts contact

🏆 Conversion Goal
Primary: Form submitted or WhatsApp chat initiated.
Secondary: User finds answer in FAQ or visits about page.

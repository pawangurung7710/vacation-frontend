🎨 UI / Design Notes (Make it Feels Premium)
🎯 Visual Hierarchy

Large typography for headlines

Strong contrast for CTA buttons

Lots of white space — let photos breathe

📸 Imagery

Always use real trekkers + landscapes
Avoid stock generic travel photos.

🧠 Interaction

Buttons: smooth hover effects (slight scale + color pop)

Menus: sticky with subtle shadow

Carousel: auto-slide but pause on hover

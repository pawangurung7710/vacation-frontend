# Product Guidelines: Vacation Nepal Trekking

## 1. Voice & Tone
- **Authoritative & Expert:** Knowledgeable and professional language regarding technical trekking details, safety, and local culture.
- **Inspiring & Evocative:** Immersive storytelling using sensory language to describe the Himalayan experience.
- **Transparent & Trustworthy:** Honest communication about costs, itineraries, and physical demands. No exaggerations.
- **Friendly & Professional:** Approachable, supportive, and globally understandable English.

## 2. Visual Identity (Himalayan Serenity Palette)
To maintain our premium positioning:
- **Background:** #F8FAFC (Clean and breathable)
- **Primary Text / Headings:** #2C4A6A (Trustworthy and professional)
- **CTA Accent:** #006D77 (Clear, modern action color)
- **Earth Accent:** #5D4037 (Used for icons/dividers to ground the design)
- **Borders:** #E0E0E0

### Visual Principles:
- **Generous Whitespace:** Avoid clutter to maintain a premium feel.
- **Modern Typography:** Large headings, clean sans-serif for body text.
- **Rounded Corners:** Use `rounded-xl` or `rounded-2xl` for cards and buttons.
- **High-Quality Imagery:** 16:9 ratio for cards, authentic trekking photos only (no generic stock).

## 3. SEO Strategy
Every page must be optimized for search without compromising readability:
- **Heading Hierarchy:** One unique `H1` per page, followed by logical `H2` and `H3` nesting.
- **Keywords:** Natural integration of terms like "Nepal trekking", "Everest Base Camp", and "local expert trek".
- **Internal Linking:** Strategic links between Listing and Detail pages to build domain authority.

## 4. Interaction Design
- **Subtle Micro-animations:** Smooth 300ms transitions.
- **Hover Effects:** Slight card lift and 2-3% image zoom.
- **Mobile First:** Ensure all complex elements (itineraries, charts) are readable on mobile.

# Initial Concept

We are building a premium, SEO-optimized trekking platform for Nepal called **Vacation Nepal Trekking**.

## Initial Phase Goal (Frontend-Only – V1)
We are building a conversion-focused marketing website, not a full booking engine yet.

### Objectives:
- Establish global trust.
- Showcase treks professionally.
- Generate high-quality inquiries (WhatsApp + form).
- Rank on Google for trekking-related keywords.
- Create scalable architecture for future backend integration.

## Success Metrics (KPIs)
- **Inquiry Conversion Rate (#1 KPI):** Measured by WhatsApp clicks, form submissions, and Trek Detail Page to inquiry conversion.
- **Organic SEO Rankings:** Ranking for key terms like "Nepal trekking", "Everest Base Camp trek", "Annapurna Circuit trek", and "Small group Nepal treks".
- **Engagement (Trek Detail Pages):** Low bounce rates on detail pages indicating high-quality itinerary layout and content structure.
- **Average Session Duration:** Tracking user interest in reading itineraries, comparing treks, and exploring charts/maps.

## Target Audience & Positioning
Vacation Nepal Trekking serves international adventure travelers across premium and well-managed budget segments who seek authentic Himalayan trekking experiences led by trusted local experts.

### Primary Audiences:
- **Premium Travelers:** Seeking enhanced comfort, safety standards, and personalized service.
- **Adventure Enthusiasts & Solo Trekkers:** Seeking expert-led, small-group expeditions.
- **Nature Lovers & Photographers:** Interested in high-altitude landscapes and cultural immersion.
- **Value-Conscious Trekkers:** Seeking transparent pricing and reliable local support without compromising safety.

### Brand Positioning:
We position ourselves as a premium yet accessible Himalayan trekking brand, offering scalable experiences — from comfortable tea-house treks to upgraded, high-service expeditions.
- **Premium but Authentic:** Not just another cheap operator.
- **Globally Credible:** High safety and quality standards across all tiers.
- **Local Expertise:** Trusted local knowledge with international service standards.

## Current Scope (V1)
1. **Homepage:** Already completed (premium marketing layout).
2. **Nepal Treks Listing Page:** Main collection page with advanced filtering, sorting, trek cards grid, SEO content, and CTAs.
3. **Trek Detail Page:** Full itinerary, altitude chart, trek map, cost breakdown, FAQ, and inquiry form.
4. **Custom Trek Page:** Personalized itinerary landing page with interactive inquiry form and WhatsApp integration.

## Out of Scope (V1)
- No payment gateway.
- No full booking checkout system.
- No user accounts.
- No admin dashboard.
- No backend API (frontend mock data only).

## Future Vision (V2+)
- Full itinerary management system.
- Backend (Spring Boot API).
- Real-time booking management.
- Review system.
- Multi-region trekking expansion.
